Selected Cases

Distressed Plant Turnaround | Minnesota

Context
Distressed $25M manufacturing plant with legacy equipment, labor attrition, and paper-based scheduling.

Action
Relocated as Interim GM. Moved onto the shop floor. Applied Lean fundamentals, rebuilt training, and transitioned planning into ERP/MRP.

Outcome
Training time reduced from 18 to 8 weeks. Inventory turns improved from 5.8x to 8.8x. Stabilized site to roughly $100K/month profit run-rate.

Saving the Integration | ArcOne

Context
$8M assembly acquisition. Founder exited on Day 1. Thousands of SKUs had no BOM data. Corporate pushed a 500-item checklist.

Action
Dropped the checklist. Focused on ERP cutover, supply continuity, and talent retention. Led manual rebuild of product structures.

Outcome
Protected the ERP cutover. Avoided shipping disruption. Hit targets 3 months early.

Regulatory Discipline | NIOSH Helmet

Context
COVID demand spike for an acquired welding helmet, but the product lacked required life-safety certification.

Action
Stopped the commercial launch despite pressure to ship. Stabilized certification path and fragile supply chain before resuming.

Outcome
Avoided major liability and recall exposure. Protected the brand. Enabled compliant market re-entry.

Operating System

1. Quality & Regulatory Risk
Stop shipments if compliance is compromised.

2. Supply Continuity
Secure fragile vendors before delivery fails.

3. Data & Systems Accuracy
Clean BOMs, stabilize ERP logic, and remove bad operating data.

4. Lean Execution
Drive direct observation, standard work, training discipline, and visual control on the floor.

5. Cost Optimization
Only after the operation is stable.

Sequence matters: stabilize, standardize, then optimize.
