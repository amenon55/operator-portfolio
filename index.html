Industrial Turnaround | $25M Manufacturing Site

Post-acquisition recovery & stabilization

The Constraint

Inherited a site with no clear ownership, outdated equipment, and a one-year backlog.

Operations were strained:

High labor turnover driven by long training cycles

Paper-based scheduling and inconsistent processes

Safety gaps identified immediately on arrival

The Fix

Addressed safety and quality gaps

Introduced structured training and clearer accountability

Shifted from batch to one-piece flow

Moved scheduling and training onto digital systems (ERP + iPads)

Diversified critical suppliers

Aligned labor structure and reduced attrition

The Value

Lead times reduced from 12 months to 6 weeks

~$500K in cost savings

Site stabilized to ~$100K/month profit run-rate

Saving the Integration | Covid

$8M assembly acquisition | ERP cutover and supply continuity

The Constraint

Founder exited on Day 1.

Thousands of SKUs had no BOM data.

Corporate pushed a 500-item checklist that was disconnected from the actual risk.

The Fix

Dropped the checklist.

Focused on ERP cutover, supply continuity, and talent retention.

Led manual rebuild of product structures to protect the transition.

The Value

Protected the ERP cutover, avoided shipping disruption, and hit integration targets three months early.

Regulatory Discipline | NIOSH Helmet

COVID demand spike | Life-safety certification risk

The Constraint

Demand surged for an acquired welding helmet, but the product lacked required NIOSH life-safety certification and the supply chain was fragile.

The Fix

Stopped the commercial launch despite pressure to ship.

Stabilized the certification path and supply chain before resuming.

The Value

Avoided major liability and recall exposure, protected the brand, and enabled compliant market re-entry.
