When to bring me in

Integration that needs running, not planning

Day 1 through stabilization. Someone who executes the integration — ERPs, people, operations — not someone who hands you another checklist.

Behind plan, exit clock ticking

The value-creation timeline is slipping. You need someone embedded who closes the gap before the deadline does.

Scaling fast, but execution keeps breaking

Growth has outrun the operating system. The team is firefighting, not running the business. Install the discipline before it breaks further.

You need a straight answer

Asset Portfolio Recovery | $25M P&L

Regional turnaround · Property management

Situation

Distressed region with high churn, weak execution cadence, and trust broken with client councils. Brought in to stabilize and rebuild.

Action

Installed weekly operating rhythm, KPI scorecards, and a fight-to-retain playbook. Consolidated 4 director roles to 2. Reset performance standards across 17 direct reports.

Outcome

Doubled EBITDA within 12 months. Retention lifted from 90% to 96%. NPS detractors cut ~50%.

Industrial Turnaround | $25M Manufacturing Site

Post-acquisition · GM assignment · Minnesota

Situation

Distressed plant mid-COVID. Owner exit, labor attrition, paper-based scheduling, aging equipment. Tasked with returning it to investable condition.

Action

Relocated onsite as GM. Replaced paper scheduling with SYSPRO ERP/MRP. Redesigned training, rebuilt the leadership bench, reset safety fundamentals.

Outcome

Site returned to ~$100K/month profit run-rate. Training time cut from 18 to 8 weeks. Inventory turns improved from 5.8x to 8.8x.

Saving the Integration | Covid

$8M assembly acquisition · ERP cutover · supply continuity

Situation

Founder exited on Day 1. Thousands of SKUs with no BOM data. Corporate issued a 500-item checklist disconnected from real risk. Border closed mid-integration.

Action

Dropped the checklist. Focused on ERP cutover, supply continuity, and talent retention. Managed the integration remotely through full COVID border restrictions.

Outcome

Integration targets hit 3 months early. No shipping disruption. ERP cutover protected.
