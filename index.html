Questions I get asked before we work together.

How much of your time do I actually get?

It depends on the engagement. I don’t bill by the hour — I work to outcomes. For fractional and interim work, we agree on scope and cadence upfront. For a full-time mandate, it’s full-time.

Will you replace my existing team?

No. I work with what’s there — figure out who’s capable and what’s blocking them, develop the ones worth developing, and address the ones who aren’t. I build the team to sustain results after I leave.

What industries do you work in?

B2B industrial — manufacturing, distribution, services. Deliberately industry-agnostic within that world. No legacy assumptions, no sector baggage. I find what works in one industry and apply it to the next.

How long does a typical engagement run?

Diagnostic: 20 minutes. Integration: 90 days to 6 months. Turnaround or interim: 6–18 months. I build toward a clear exit — the goal is that you don’t need me anymore.

What do you need from leadership to be effective?

A real mandate and real authority. And a sponsor who won’t get confused when existing managers push back.

Do you need a large team to deliver results?

No. I run lean and frugal. One operator who gets close to the work delivers faster than a consulting team, without the overhead.
